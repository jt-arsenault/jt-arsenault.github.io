Writing Samples

Science for the Public News Article
This article summarizes and interview I conducted with Dr. Xiangfei Qia, a professor in UMass Amherst's department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. Dr. Xia summarizes his lab's work on memrisoters, a once-purely theoretical type of resistor capable of "remembering" the electrical impulses that have recently passed through it.

2018 Plant Biology Symposium
Summary of the Graduate Symposium on Plant Biology hosted at UMass in October of 2018. The theme of the symposium was "Tree Genes to Ecosystem Function."

Glimmers From the Bright World
Long-form blog post/article on the medicinal promise of ketamine as an antidepressant, framed in the context of Marcia Moore's 1978 book Journeys Into the Bright World. The book describes Moore's experiences injecting herself with ketamine hydrochloride at various dosages, accompanied by her astrological/new age interpretations of the effects.

Updated and Re-styled PrismHR Task Sheet (PDF)
One of the help sheets I reorganized and re-styled during my 2018 summer internship at PrismHR. The images were extracted from the original help sheet's pdf file, while the information was transferred and then edited/fact-checked within MadCap Flare.
