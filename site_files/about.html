About Me

I see myself as a bridge between the worlds of technical writing and scientific research. Tech writers will tell you that they're passionate about clarity of composition and user-friendly design. Scientists will tell you about the critical importance of problem-solving and critical thinking. My goal is to unite the best aspects of these fields to generate content that is intuitive, organized, informative, and well-researched.

I have degrees from UMass Amherst in both Biochemistry and Technical Writing. Click here to learn more about my educational and professional experience.
